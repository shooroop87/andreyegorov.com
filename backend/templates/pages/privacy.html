1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Andrey Egorov ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit andreyegorov.com (the "Website") or use my services.

2. Information I Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you contact me or request services, I may collect: name and contact information (email, phone number), company name and business details, payment information (processed securely through third-party providers), and project requirements and communication history.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Website, I automatically collect: IP address and approximate location, browser type and device information, pages visited and time spent on the Website, and referring website or source.

3. Analytics Services

3.1 Google Analytics

I use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the Website. Google Analytics collects only the IP address assigned to you on the date you visit, rather than your name or other identifying information. You can opt-out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3.2 Yandex Metrica

I also use Yandex Metrica for analytics purposes. For more information, please visit Yandex Metrica Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

The Website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Types of cookies used: essential cookies (required for the Website to function), analytics cookies (help understand how visitors use the Website), and preference cookies (remember your settings like dark/light mode). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Payment Processing

For payment processing, I use third-party payment providers including T-Bank (Tinkoff). Payment card details are processed directly by the payment provider. I do not store your full credit card number. Transactions are secured using industry-standard encryption.

6. How I Use Your Information

I use collected information to: provide and improve my services, respond to your inquiries and requests, process payments and send invoices, send project updates and relevant communications, analyze Website usage and improve user experience, and comply with legal obligations.

7. Data Sharing

I do not sell your personal information. I may share your data with: payment processors to complete transactions, analytics providers (Google, Yandex) as described above, and legal authorities when required by law.

8. Data Retention

I retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Contact and project information is typically retained for 5 years after the last interaction.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to: access the personal information I hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, object to or restrict processing of your data, and data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact me at job@andreyegorov.com.

10. Data Security

I implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

11. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including Russia and countries within the European Union. I ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

12. Children's Privacy

The Website is not intended for children under 16. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

13. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
